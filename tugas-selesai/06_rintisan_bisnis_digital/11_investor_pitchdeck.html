Mitigation Strategy:

  • • Maintain close relationships with regulatory bodies
  • • Exceed current compliance requirements
  • • Legal advisory board with healthcare law experts
  • • Flexible platform architecture for rapid adaptation

Therapist Shortage

Limited pool of qualified hypnotherapists in Indonesia

Mitigation Strategy:

  • • Establish CUR-HEART Certification Academy
  • • Partner with international training institutes
  • • Develop comprehensive training curriculum
  • • Competitive compensation & career development

Market Education

Low awareness of hypnotherapy benefits among target market

Mitigation Strategy:

  • • Comprehensive educational content marketing
