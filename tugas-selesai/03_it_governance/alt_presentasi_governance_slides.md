## PRESENTATION NOTES & TALKING POINTS

### Opening (Slides 1-4):
- Start dengan strong business case - emphasize competitive advantage
- Highlight urgency - digital transformation imperative
- Connect IT governance dengan business success stories
- Set expectation untuk practical, implementable solution

### Assessment Results (Slides 5-7):
- Use visual aids untuk demonstrate current state gaps
- Emphasize risk exposure dengan concrete examples
- Show clear connection antara governance gaps dan business impact
- Build sense of urgency untuk immediate action

### Solution Presentation (Slides 8-12):
- Focus pada proven frameworks (COBIT, ITIL) - not experimental
- Emphasize practical implementation approach
- Show clear timeline dengan realistic milestones
- Address potential concerns proactively

### Financial Justification (Slides 12-13):
- Lead dengan strong ROI story - 134% annual return
- Break down benefits into tangible categories
- Compare investment dengan cost of doing nothing
- Emphasize both financial dan strategic returns

### Implementation Strategy (Slides 14-17):
- Address implementation risks upfront
- Show comprehensive risk mitigation
- Emphasize phased approach untuk minimize disruption
- Highlight success factors dan dependencies

### Call to Action (Slides 18-21):
- Create urgency untuk immediate decision
- Provide clear next steps
- Address potential objections
- End dengan confidence dalam success

### Presentation Delivery Tips:
1. **Executive Audience**: Focus pada business impact, ROI, strategic advantage
2. **Technical Audience**: Emphasize framework rigor, implementation details
3. **Mixed Audience**: Balance business case dengan technical credibility
4. **Time Management**: Allow adequate time untuk Q&A dan discussion
5. **Visual Impact**: Use charts, diagrams untuk complex concepts
6. **Storytelling**: Connect technical governance concepts dengan business scenarios

### Common Questions & Answers:
**Q: Why COBIT 2019 over other frameworks?**
A: Industry standard, comprehensive coverage, proven track record, vendor-neutral

**Q: Can we implement incrementally?**
A: Yes, our 4-phase approach allows progressive implementation dengan immediate benefits

**Q: What if we don't have internal expertise?**
A: Plan includes external consulting support dan comprehensive training program

**Q: How do we measure success?**
A: Clear KPIs, regular maturity assessments, performance dashboards, stakeholder feedback

**Q: What's the risk of not implementing?**
A: Continued risk exposure, competitive disadvantage, compliance issues, missed opportunities
